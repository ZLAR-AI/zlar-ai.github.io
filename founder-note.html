Founder Note

I built ZLAR because AI is learning to create consequence faster than institutions are learning to create standing.

ZLAR gives humans standing at the moment machine intelligence tries to affect the world. It is a witnessed execution boundary between intelligence and consequence.

Contact ZLAR See a sample receipt

AI is no longer just generating language. It is generating consequence: files changed, tools called, workflows triggered, records moved, people affected. That shift changes the governance question.

The question is not only whether the model is useful. It is whether a human still has standing at the moment the model's work tries to cross into the world.

The lock on the door changes how sleep feels.

That is the emotional shape of ZLAR. Not fear of intelligence. Not theatrical resistance to acceleration. A lock that lets you rest because consequence has a structure outside the thing trying to act.

Relaxed acceleration

Teams move faster when consequence has structure. The work is not to slow machine intelligence down for its own sake. The work is to give delegation a boundary, so the human can stop scanning every shadow and start deciding where authority actually belongs.

ZLAR lets routed actions cross signed deterministic policy. Policy can allow, deny, or ask a human. Every governed decision leaves evidence. That is relaxed acceleration: more room to move because the dangerous path is no longer informal.

Human standing

The phrase matters. Human standing is not a vague preference for human oversight. It means there is a place where a human can still say yes, no, wait, prove it, or show me the record.

Affected people need that too. The person whose file changed, claim moved, record updated, workflow blocked, or account shifted may never have been in the approval loop. The receipt gives them a question they can ask: what happened, under what authority, and can the record be verified?

The boundary

ZLAR is built around a simple object: a governed path from intelligence to consequence. The agent may plan, write, request, and call. Consequence crosses through a separate authority.

The future needs places where humanity can still stand. ZLAR is one working form of that place.

Exact scope

  • ZLAR governs routed/intercepted action surfaces only.
  • Safe Codex wording: "ZLAR can govern Codex CLI-invoked MCP tool calls when those MCP servers are routed through ZLAR."
  • Unrouted shell/filesystem/browser/app/network/model-reasoning/final-text surfaces are not claimed as governed by the current proof path.
  • /contest is not implemented.
  • External non-Vincent verifier attestation remains prepared/pending unless state changes.
